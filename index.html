WHAT WE OFFER

OUR SERVICES

Tilicho Consulting Group aims to provide One Stop Solution to your Education & Visa requirements. We will be there to assist you throughout your journey to your preferred destination.

University and College Admission

Choosing an Educational Institution can be a daunting task considering the availability of thousands of Universities and Colleges in your preferred destinations. You need to consider various factors such as credibility of Educational institutions, recognition from the governing authorities, cost of the course, Eligibility requirements , career outcome and possible Migration pathways. We have a team of Expert Educational Counselors and Registered Migration Agents who can help you to choose the right course, institution and the Location based on your requirements and eligibility.

Migration and Visa Services

Tilicho Consulting Group is an Australian Visa and Migration Consultancy firm led by experienced Registered Migration Agents who have experience in assisting thousands of clients for their desired visa and migration goals in Australia.

Professional Year (PY) Programs

The Professional Year program is a Job-ready program designed for graduates. There are three streams in the Professional Year program.

Internship facilitation

At Tilicho Consulting Group we are committed to help our clients achieve their career goals. We have collaborated with various organizations to help our clients to join training and internship programs which will help them to pursue the career of their choice.

English Preparation Classes: IELTS and PTE

English language tests are mandatory requirements for the majority of visa applicants. They play a critical role in a point-based migration system to meet the par score to be eligible or have a high point to have a chance to get the invitation.

Health Insurance: OSHC and OVHC

As an Australian temporary visa holder you should consider taking out appropriate Health Insurance. It might be one of the mandatory requirements attached to your visa conditions.
